# 0002: Content sourced from public data, not original analysis

The knowledge base will structure and present publicly available Taiwan stock market data (TWSE rules, TPEx listings, MOPS filings, FSC regulations) without original investment analysis or strategy curation. This makes the content objective and verifiable against authoritative sources, but sacrifices proprietary insight.

**Considered options:**
- **Original curation (A)** — highest differentiation, but requires the author to be a domain expert and produce ongoing analysis
- **Hybrid (C)** — public data as foundation + original strategy layer; more work than pure public data but retains uniqueness
- **Public data only (B, chosen)** — fastest to build, zero editorial risk, content is always factually grounded in source documents

**Consequences:** the site becomes a reference/encyclopedia rather than an advisory platform. Competitive differentiation is weak (anyone can access TWSE data), so success depends on presentation quality and usability — better search, better structure, better cross-referencing than the official sites.
