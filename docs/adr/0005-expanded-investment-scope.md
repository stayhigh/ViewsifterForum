# 0005: Content scope expanded beyond passive investing

The knowledge base now covers multiple investment approaches, not just passive indexing (0050 DCA). The scope includes situational strategies such as lump-sum buying during market corrections, cash-reserve management, and conditional entry rules.

**Context:** the project started with a focus on passive investing, but the author wants the knowledge base to serve as a reference for different market conditions — not just the "always buy and hold" script.

**Decision:** add content covering:
- Passive DCA (0050/006208)
- Market correction tactics (lump-sum conditions, cash reserve ratios)
- Sector rotation / thematic investing (when appropriate)
- Risk management (position sizing, stop conditions)

Each approach is presented as a structured strategy with clear conditions, not as investment advice.

**Consequences:** broader scope = more content to curate. The unified entry schema (ADR-0003) already supports this — strategies can be tagged and cross-referenced with market data entries.
